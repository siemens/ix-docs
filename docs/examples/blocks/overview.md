## What are blocks?

Unlike components, blocks are not versioned and not automatically updated in your application. They are starting points for your development and can easily be installed in your project using the iX blocks CLI.

Every block is:

- made of multiple iX components
- reviewed and approved by iX designers & developers
- best‑practice aligned in layout, interaction, accessibility, and behavior
- copy‑ready, so you can quickly adapt it for your use case
- evolving and updated over time

## How does that relate to UX Design Patterns?

- **UX Design Patterns** define the conceptual, reusable solution to a user problem, independent of any design system.
- **UI Components** are the technical building blocks used to construct interfaces.
- **Blocks sit in the middle**: they are ready-made compositions of multiple components that demonstrate the implementation.

Patterns guide the intent. Blocks show how to build it. Components are the raw materials.

## What is Siemens ix-cli?

The Siemens ix-cli is a command-line interface tool that allows you to easily add iX blocks to your project. With a simple command, you can quickly integrate pre-built blocks into your application, saving you time and effort in development.
