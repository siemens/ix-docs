# Progress update  

## General rules


Use sentence case to align with our UX writing guidelines with a horizontal ellipsis (…) without a space. 

<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Downloading assets…</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Downloading Assets...</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use the present simple progressive (continuous, -ing) verb form to indicate ongoing actions. 

<DoAndDont>
  <DoAndDont.Do>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Downloading…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Charging…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Saving…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Searching…</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Do>
  <DoAndDont.Dont>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Been loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>To be loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Will be loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use verbs before nouns to focus on the action and make messages easier to scan.  

<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Loading file…</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>File loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>


Use the Unicode (U+2026) instead of adding three full stops (Mac Option+/Windows ALT+0133). 
<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Loading…</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Loading ...</DoAndDont.Item>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Processing⋰</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use ellipses for text with numbers or the object to show a running process. 

<DoAndDont>
  <DoAndDont.Do>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Uploading 5 items…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Uploading items…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Processing request…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Saving settings…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Saving changes…</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Do>
  <DoAndDont.Dont>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Uploading 1 file</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Avoid ellipses to show time remaining and approximate or unknown times as they’re status indicators, not actions.  

<DoAndDont>
  <DoAndDont.Do>
    <DoAndDont.Item>5 minutes remaining</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>About 33 minutes remaining</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Do>
  <DoAndDont.Dont>
    <DoAndDont.Item>5 minutes remaining…</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>About 33 minutes remaining…</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Change verb form without ellipses when possible in buttons with loading spinners to avoid visual clutter.  

<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Download → Downloading → Downloaded</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Download → Downloading… → Downloaded</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use the correct verb form without using ellipses to show background processes when possible.  

<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Download → Downloading → Downloaded</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Download → Downloading… → Downloaded</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use specific and definitive text to make users aware of what is happening and how long the process takes.  

<DoAndDont>
	<DoAndDont.Do>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Downloading 5 files…</DoAndDont.Item>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Estimated time remaining: 3 minutes 45 seconds</DoAndDont.Item>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Time remaining: 2 minutes 30 seconds</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Do>
	<DoAndDont.Dont>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Please wait…</DoAndDont.Item>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Thank you for waiting…</DoAndDont.Item>
		<DoAndDont.Item>Just a moment.</DoAndDont.Item>
	</DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Use informative text and actions when multiple processes are happening at the same time.   

<DoAndDont>
  <DoAndDont.Do>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Carrying out tasks…</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Do>
  <DoAndDont.Dont>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Thank you for waiting.</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Just a moment.</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Dont>
</DoAndDont>

Manage user expectations and reduce frustration for lengthy processes with unknown timelines.  

<DoAndDont>
  <DoAndDont.Do>
    <DoAndDont.Item>Processing your request. This usually takes about 1–2 minutes.</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>This step takes a bit longer than usual.</DoAndDont.Item>
    <DoAndDont.Item>We're preparing your dashboard. Once it's ready, you'll be able to customize your view.</DoAndDont.Item>
  </DoAndDont.Do>
</DoAndDont>
