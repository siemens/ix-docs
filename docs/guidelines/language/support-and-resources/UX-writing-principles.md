![Trust](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-10143&t=XEKsbqtBj1TvyDOK-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We increase confidence and remove doubt.<br/><br/>We help our users with fast and secure decision-making. | Users make high-risk decisions at work, e.g., initiate or postpone maintenance.<br/><br/>Users need predictable interactions that create trust in the product as well as the brand. | We write with the correct level of urgency (warnings, alarms, errors, and consequences).<br/><br/>We write with familiarity to remove the memory burden and prevent errors.<br/><br/>We provide clear constraints so users can trust their actions, their consequences, and the product. |


![Accuracy](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-11468&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Our language is specific and concise.<br/><br/>We ensure users understand the words in the same way.<br/><br/>We leave no room for interpretation or guessing. | Users cannot have room for interpretation or misunderstanding.<br/><br/>Users cannot have doubt as to the meaning of the word.<br/><br/>Users cannot have any doubt as to the consequences of their actions. | We do user research to become familiar with user terminology.<br/><br/>We use precise, true, explicit, and exact wording.<br/><br/>We perform UX wording reviews and collaborate with designers.<br/><br/>We embed the UX writer role and activities into products and teams.<br/><br/>We provide top-down and bottom-up support and guidance. |


![Consistency](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7718-93&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We write with uniformity across screens, apps, and products from Siemens.<br/><br/>Users can rely on our writing to be the same across screens and products.<br/><br/>We write with the same voice and tone. | Users don't have time to get used to changes or variations when carrying out tasks in their daily work.<br/><br/>Users should not have to wonder whether different words, situations, or actions mean the same thing. | We ensure the same or similar contexts have the same language.<br/><br/>We follow platform and industry conventions.<br/><br/>We follow Siemens voice and tone guidelines. |


![Efficiency](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7718-94&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We move users through their daily work tasks easily and quickly.<br/><br/>We move users through high-risk and exceptional tasks rapidly.<br/><br/>Users move through our products logically and find our writing intuitive. | Users within industrial settings need immediate feedback.<br/><br/>Users within industrial settings need to filter information quickly.<br/><br/>Users within industrial settings need to make decisions quickly. | We make language scannable and balanced.<br/><br/>We offer contextual help and reduce information users must remember.<br/><br/>We present the right words at the right time, or not at all. |


![Human](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-11617&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We create products with engaging and supportive interaction.<br/><br/>We have authentic conversations with our users.<br/><br/>We write products that sound human. | Users need human-centric language based on their needs and behaviors.<br/><br/>Our products need to match our users' way of thinking and working. | We avoid jargon, or use only the technical jargon required.<br/><br/>We design end-to-end conversations for tasks. |


![Empathy](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-11631&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We put ourselves in the users' shoes.<br/><br/>We anticipate their confusion and pain points.<br/><br/>We understand what our users want, need, and are engaged by. | Users want products that understand their requirements.<br/><br/>We genuinely care about the people we design for.<br/><br/>We need to write according to our users' emotional state.<br/><br/>Industrial application users represent more sensitive and urgent situations. | We conduct user and product research.<br/><br/>We use personas, including pain points and needs.<br/><br/>We use user journeys that include their daily tasks.<br/><br/>We do iterative UX writing reviews.<br/><br/>We apologize when appropriate. |


![Accessibility](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-11645&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We write for everyone, whatever their expertise or background.<br/><br/>We write for maximum accessibility. | Users have varying literacy skills in all levels of life.<br/><br/>Users have cognitive, visual, and motor impairments.<br/><br/>Users, regardless of ability or circumstance, must get the information they need. | We follow our company's voice and tone guidelines.<br/><br/>We write at the correct literacy level for our users.<br/><br/>We implement WCAG principles. |


![Branding](https://www.figma.com/design/wEptRgAezDU1z80Cn3eZ0o/iX-Documentation-illustrations?node-id=7675-11659&t=VYAOKhJMR0BCK4c4-4)
| What | Why | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| We follow product principles.<br/><br/>We follow the company brand principles.<br/><br/>We speak the brand. | Users need to know they are using a product from our company.<br/><br/>Products need to feel familiar to users.<br/><br/>We want users to recognize and recommend our products. | We apply UX writing guidelines.<br/><br/>We use our company's voice and tone principles. |
