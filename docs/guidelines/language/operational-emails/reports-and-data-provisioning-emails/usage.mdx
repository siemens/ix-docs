Use these rules alongside the [General rules](../overview-operational-emails/usage.mdx#general-rules). All placeholders are shown with square brackets, e.g. [placeholder]. We have used [App name] as the standard placeholder for you to adapt individually to your product.
:::

## Subject lines

Use precise and descriptive subject lines that immediately convey the email's purpose.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | Production efficiency report October 2025 available</li>
    <li>[App name] | Test report available</li>
    <li>[App name] | CSV import complete</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

## Body text

Use the full name of the report, dataset or data type being imported or migrated.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>The monthly "Q3 2025 energy consumption optimization report" is now available.</li>
    <li>The import of your "Product inventory CSV" is complete.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Your report is ready.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include a brief summary or key highlights from the report, especially if it's a regular deliverable.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>This month's "Performance overview" highlights a 5% improvement in uptime for production line 2 due to recent sensor recalibration.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include the reporting period or relevant dates for the data covered in the report.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>The "Asset utilization report" for November 1–15, 2025 has been generated and is ready for your review.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include direct links to the report or data, minimizing steps for the user.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Your "Predictive maintenance analytics report" is available.<br/>Button: [Open report]</li>
    <li>The migrated "historical maintenance logs" are now fully accessible within Maintenance history.<br/>Button: [Open Maintenance history]</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use "you" and "your" for information or actions that are recipient-specific. Use neutral wording for shared or generally available information.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>The monthly "Energy consumption report" is now available.</li>
    <li>Your requested "Product inventory CSV" has been imported and is available to download.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>
