## Subject lines

Use a subject line that accurately reflects the email's content and purpose.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | New Terms of Use</li>
    <li>[App name] | Changes to your data options</li>
    <li>[App name] | Join our user testing</li>
    <li>[App name] | Tell us about your app experience</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

## Body text

### Agreements and confirmation

Be specific when asking for confirmation or agreement.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>You have until January 31, 2026 to confirm the new Terms of Use.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
<ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Reading this email means you agree to our new Terms of Use.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include all relevant details of the agreement, order or change for the customer's records.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Your order for 5 "SINAMICS G120 Drives" (Order #QuantumShift Platform-2025-12345) has been received and processed. Estimated delivery: January 15, 2026.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Avoid ambiguous phrasing that could lead to misunderstandings.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Your support contract for "Automation System S7-1500" has been renewed for one year, effective January 1, 2026.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
<ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>We've extended your S7-1500 support.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include a method for the customer to review, modify or cancel the agreement or action if applicable.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>You can review your subscription details or manage your preferences at any time through your [App name] account dashboard: [link to dashboard].</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Feedback and surveys

Use clear and polite invitations that explain the purpose of the survey.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>We value your opinion and would appreciate feedback on your recent interaction with our support team.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include an estimate of the time required to complete the survey.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>This short survey will take approximately 5 minutes to complete.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include direct and secure links or buttons to make participation easier.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Share your thoughts via this secure link: [Link to survey].</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
<ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Go to our website to find the survey.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Communication and contact

Include specific reference numbers or identifiers to help users track issues.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>When contacting support, reference ticket ID: [App name]-2026-54321.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Avoid hiding (or making it difficult) to find options for managing communication preferences or unsubscribing.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Manage your email preferences here: [link to preference center].</li>
    <li>Unsubscribe from emails here.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>
