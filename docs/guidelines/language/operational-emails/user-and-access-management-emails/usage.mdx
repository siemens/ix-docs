## Subject lines

State the clear purpose or topic of the email.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>[App name] | Welcome to [App name]</li>
<li>[App name] | Log in from new device</li>
<li>[App name] | Notification of role change</li>
<li>[App name] | One-time PIN for verification</li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

## Body text

Start with a clear sentence describing the topic of the email.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>Thank you for choosing [App name].</li>
<li>Your account has been successfully created.</li>
<li>You requested a one-time PIN to verify your identity.</li>
<li>A new device has been registered to this account: [{'user@example.com'}]</li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

Provide users with additional information or a summary.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>What you need to do<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Open [App name]<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Start configuring your dashboard<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Explore key features: Real-time energy monitoring, load optimization and predictive analytics

	&nbsp;&nbsp;Button: Open<br/></li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>The information below relates to your newly registered device<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Device name: Laptop123<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Device ID: ABCDEFGHI<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Operating system: Windows<br/>
	&nbsp;&nbsp;&bull; Date: November 11, 2026, 14:32<br/></li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

Provide users with clear next steps or instructions.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>You are just a few steps away from starting your [App name] journey. To get started, register for an [App name] ID using your email address and create a strong, secure password.</li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

Never include sensitive information such as passwords directly in the email.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>To set your password securely, visit the [App name] account portal.</li>

</ul>
</div>
<div className="donts" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
<li>Here is your default password: 12345 – change it here!</li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>

Mention both previous and new states when access or role settings are changed.

<div className="dos-and-donts" markdown="true">
<div className="dos" markdown="true">
<ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
<li>There have been changes to your role for [App name]<br/>
	New role: Administrator<br/>
	Previous role: Operator</li>

</ul>
</div>
</div>
