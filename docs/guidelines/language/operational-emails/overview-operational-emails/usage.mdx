# Usage

:::note

All placeholders are shown with square brackets, e.g. [placeholder]. We have used [App name] as the standard placeholder for you to adapt individually to your product.
:::


## General rules

Keep emails short, add relevant information and avoid complex and lengthy sentences.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Renew your license before December 31, 2026.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>It is recommended by us that you renew your license before December 31 avoiding a possible delay in service.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use past tense for completed events.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Payment was processed Tuesday, November 11, 2025.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Payment is processing Tuesday, November 11, 2025.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use future tense for upcoming actions or scheduled events.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>We will deploy firmware Version 5.3 on November 22.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>We deploy firmware Version 5.3 on November 22.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use the present perfect passive tense (have been / done) for system-generated confirmations or assignments.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>You have been assigned the role of "Energy Analyst".</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>The system assigned you the role of "Energy Analyst".</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use the past active tense when someone manually assigns a task or role.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>John Smith assigned the following task to you:</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>You have been assigned the following task by John Smith.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use sentence case in your emails (capitalize only the first word of a sentence or heading and any proper nouns).

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>What's happening</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>What's Happening</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use typographic double quotation marks for opening quotes " (Unicode U+201C) and for closing quotes " (Unicode U+201D) instead of single marks for consistency.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>You have been assigned the role of "Energy Analyst".</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>You have been assigned the role of 'Energy Analyst'.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

## Subject lines

Start the subject line with the application name. Use pipe separators to clearly structure the subject line, and separate the product name, action and topic.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | Version 2.0 available</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>New Version 2.0 available</li>
    <li>[App name]: Version 2.0 available</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include purpose and theme, avoiding generic or vague subject lines.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | Scheduled maintenance November 10, 2026, at 11:00</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>[App name] | Maintenance soon</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use language that reflects the urgency of the email and mention if action is required.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | Immediate action required | Security vulnerability fix</li>
    <li>[App name] | No action required | New events detected</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>[App name] | Security issues</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Keep the subject line as short as possible. Additional information should be provided in the body text.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[App name] | Immediate action required | Update</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>[App name] | Immediate action required | Update from old Version 2.4 to new Version 2.5 due to security issues</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

## Body text

Start with a polite salutation using "Dear" followed by a comma (American English). Do not use gendered titles or gendered forms of address. Use the recipient's name if available. If no name is available, use a neutral greeting. For German translations, Siemens AG employees and partners can follow the [German Brand Guide](https://brandville.siemens.com/en/design-elements/language/inklusive-und-gendergerechte-sprache) for inclusive language.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Dear [App name] user,</li>
    <li>Dear John Schmidt,</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Hi</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Repeat key words from the subject line in the body text.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Subject line: [App name] | Version 2.0 available<br/>
  Body text: We've released Version 2.0 of [App name].</li>
    <li>Subject line: [App name] | One-time PIN for verification<br/>
  Body text: You requested a one-time PIN to verify your identity.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use clear headings for users to easily scan body text for important information (omit the question mark).

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Heading: What happened</li>
    <li>Heading: What's happening</li>
    <li>Heading: What you need to do</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include a professional and polite closing with a descriptive signature.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Kind regards,<br/>
  Your [App name] team</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Best wishes,<br/>
  Your friendly [App name] team</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Time-related information

Include specific times, dates and time zones whenever relevant.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>The update will start automatically on November 10 between 02:00–04:00 CET (UTC+1).</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>The update will be next week.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Include duration and instructions when applicable.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>The registration link is valid for 24 hours.</li>
    <li>The PIN is valid for 15 minutes from the time you received this email.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Actions and links

Include a clear message when users don't have to do anything.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>What you need to do<br/>
  You don't need to do anything. We'll inform you when the data migration is complete.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Provide a clear call-to-action when user actions are needed.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>What you need to do<br/>
  Restart your system after the update to ensure all changes take effect.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Add minimal links, actions, or buttons in each email (we recommend one per email).

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>[Open App name] event list</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

If possible, provide call-to-actions in buttons instead of links.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Button: Open dashboard</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use inline text links for additional information.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>For additional information, see [webpage].</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Use a full, live link when buttons are not possible in the email (see [External links and resources](../../menu-functions-and-ui-labels/external-links-and-resources.md)).

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Open your dashboard</li>
    <li>[App name].com/dashboard</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>{'https://example.com/'}[App name]</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Responding options

Include options for users to respond to or get support when possible.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>If you have questions or need support, contact us at [email address].</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Clearly state when an email is automated and cannot be replied to.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>This email is automated: Do not reply.</li>
    <li>This is an automated email. Do not reply.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

### Disclaimer and footer

Check the correct legal text with your legal team and include a legal disclaimer, the company's legal footer, and any other required legal information.<br/>


### Formatting

Highlight important information but avoid excessive and mixed text formatting.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Review the attached maintenance report for <u>details</u>.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>Review the attached **Maintenance Report** for details.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>

Do not use emojis.

<div className="dos-and-donts">
<div className="dos">
  <ul aria-label="Recommended practices">
    <li>Welcome to [App name] – your energy management platform</li>
  </ul>
</div>
<div className="donts">
  <ul aria-label="Practices to avoid">
    <li>We love you for joining us!!!! Welcome to the team ❤️ ❤️</li>
  </ul>
</div>
</div>
